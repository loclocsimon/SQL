# E-Commerce Revenue and Customer Behavior Analysis

## Objective
- Analyze transactional E-commerce data to uncover revenue drivers, customer concentration, seasonal trends, product performance, and geographic demand in order to provide actionable business recommendations for growth and retention.

## Dataset
- Source: Public Online Retail Dataset (Kaggle)
- Size: 5,942 unique customers, 19.2 million dollars in total revenue
- Description: Transactional order level data including customer ID, invoice details, product descriptions, quantity, unit price, country, and invoice date

## Tools Used
- How many unique customers are there and how many purchases were made by the top 10 buyers?
- Who are the top revenue generating customers and where are they located?
- What is the total revenue and which year performed best?
- Are we growing and how many products were sold each year?
- Is revenue seasonal and which months generate the most income?
- What are the top 10 revenue driving products?
- What is the Average Order Value and what does it reveal about buying behavior?
- Where does demand originate geographically and what percentage does each region contribute?


## Key Insights
- 10 customers, 17% percent of the customer base, generated 14% percent of total revenue, indicating strong revenue concentration.
- The United Kingdom contributed approximately 85 percent of total sales, highlighting geographic dependency.
- Revenue and order volume peaked between September and December, confirming strong seasonality.
- DOTCOM POSTAGE had the highest Average Order Value at 223.44 compared to the overall average of 58.30.


## Recommendations
- Develop a high value customer retention program with loyalty incentives and exclusive benefits.
- Align marketing campaigns with peak seasonal months and introduce promotions during slower periods to stabilize revenue.
- Implement localized pricing and upsell strategies in top performing countries to increase Average Revenue Per User.
- Test product bundling strategies pairing high AOV products with complementary items to increase cart value.

## Deliverables
- SQL queries used for customer, revenue, seasonal, and product analysis
- Cleaned dataset with validated revenue calculations
- Business insights report with strategic recommendations
